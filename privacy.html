Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-11

LottoPicks is designed to work primarily on-device. This policy explains what information is (and is not) collected.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information.

  • No account required
  • No name, email, phone number, or location collected by the app
  • No selling or sharing of personal data

On-Device Data

If you save tickets or favorites, that information is stored locally on your device (e.g., using iOS local storage). Uninstalling the app may remove this local data.

Analytics & Tracking

LottoPicks does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in its current version. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated accordingly.
